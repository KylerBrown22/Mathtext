\((x + 1)^3\) Formula

Formula: $(x + 1)^3 = x^3 + 3x^2 + 3x + 1$

Proof of \((x + 1)^3\) formula

The \((x + 1)^3\) formula can be verified or proved by multiplying $(x + 1)$ three times $$ \begin{array}{rcl} (x + 1)^3 & = & (x + 1)(x + 1)(x + 1)\\ (x + 1)^3 & = & [x^2 + x + x + 1](x + 1)\\ & = & ( x + 1)[ x^2 + 2x + 1]\\ & = & x^3 + 2x^2 + x^2 + 2x + 1\\ & = & x^3 + 3x^2 + 3x + 1\\ \,\textrm{Therefore,}\, (x + 1)^3 & = & x^3 + 3x^2 + 3x + 1 \end{array}$$

Example of \((x + 1)^3\) formula

Simplify $(2x + 1)^3$ using $(x + 1)^3$ expansion formula $$ \begin{array}{rcl} Using (x + 1)^3 expansion fromula:\\ (x + 1)^3 = x^3 + 3x^2 + x + 1\\ \,\textrm{Comparing and putting formulas:}\,\\ (2x + 1) & = & (2x)^3 + 3(2x)^2 + 2x + 1\\ & = & 8x^3 + 12x^2 + 2x + 1\\ (2x + 3)^3 & = & 8x^3 + 12x^2 + 2x + 1\\ \end{array}$$
